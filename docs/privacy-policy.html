CalSnap - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Publisher: Heldig Lab

Data Collection

CalSnap does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data stays on your device.

Data Stored Locally

CalSnap stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Food log entries and calorie tracking data
  • App preferences and settings
  • Purchase status (premium unlock)

Advertising

CalSnap uses Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

In-App Purchases

CalSnap offers in-app purchases processed by Google Play (Android) or the App Store (iOS). No personal payment information is collected by the app.

No Accounts

No login, signup, or personal information is required.

Children's Privacy

CalSnap is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected on this page.
